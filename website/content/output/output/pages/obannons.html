O'Bannon's Favorite: Irish Car Bomb

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 ounce Baileys Irish cream
  • 1/2 ounce Irish whiskey
  • Guinness beer

Instructions:

  1. Add the Baileys and whiskey to a shot glass.
  2. ADrop the shot into a half-pint of the beer.
