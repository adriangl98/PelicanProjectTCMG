Mama Sake

Mama Sake's Favorite: Sake Bomb

Ingredients:

  • 1 pint of light beer
  • 1/2 ounce Irish whiskey
  • Guinness beer

Instructions:

  1. Pour beer into mug
  2. Place chopstick on top of mug
  3. Pour the sake in a shot glass and place it on top of the chopsticks
  4. Bang the table until the shot falls in the mug
  5. Chug !
