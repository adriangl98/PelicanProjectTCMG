Dry Bean's Favorite: Kamikaze Shot

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 3/4 oz Orange liqueur
  • 3/4 oz Fresh lime juice

Instructions:

  1. Makes 2 shots.
  2. Add all the ingredients into a shaker with ice, and shake thoroughly until well-chilled.
  3. Strain into two shot glasses.
